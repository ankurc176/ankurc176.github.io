Cookie settings

Marimekko collects data with the help of cookies and similar technologies to provide the best possible user experience, target content and advertising, analyze usage and provide social media features. We may share the data with our marketing and other relevant partners. By selecting "Accept all cookies", you agree to the use of cookies and similar technologies. You can refuse the use of non-essential cookies by selecting "Accept strictly necessary cookies only". You can withdraw or change your consent at any time in the cookie settings.

Strictly Necessary

Always Active

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you which amount to a request for services, such as setting your privacy preferences, logging in, or filling in forms. You can set your browser to block or alert you about these cookies, but some parts of the site will not work optimally. These cookies do not store any personally identifiable information.

Performance

These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site. They help us to know which pages or features are the most and least popular and see how visitors move around the site. All of these cookies set an ID specific to the cookie that can connect website interactions and subsequent sessions belonging to the same browser during the cookie lifetime. If you have registered and logged in as a Marimekko customer, some of these cookies also store a hashed persistent identifier referring to your customer account. This enables connecting your website interactions with your customer account and serve you a better experience. If you do not allow these cookies, we will not know when you have visited our site, and will not be able to monitor its performance.

Functional

These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalisation. They may be set by us or by third party providers whose services we have added to our pages. If you do not allow these cookies then some or all of these services may not function properly.

Marketing

These cookies may be set through our site by our third-party advertising service providers. They may be used by those service providers to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant adverts on other sites. They do not store directly personal information, but are based on uniquely identifying your browser and internet device. If you do not allow these cookies, you will experience less targeted advertising.
